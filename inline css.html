About Nature

Nature is the most beautiful gift on Earth. It includes mountains, rivers, forests, oceans, plants, and animals. Nature provides us with fresh air, water, food, and peace of mind.

Importance of Nature

  • Provides oxygen and clean air
  • Maintains ecological balance
  • Supports wildlife and biodiversity
  • Gives peace and relaxation

Protect Nature

We must protect nature by planting more trees, reducing pollution, saving water, and protecting wildlife. Nature is essential for the survival of all living beings.
