Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in developing India's missile technology and nuclear capabilities. He inspired millions of young people through his vision, leadership, and dedication to education and innovation.

Achievements

  • Known as the "Missile Man of India".
  • Played a key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007).
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna in 1997.

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
